How to Use

This calculator helps you estimate the real USD amount received from galvanized coil sales after deducting the exchange transfer fee.
  • 1
    Enter the current USD Rate in Toman.
  • 2
    Enter the Exchange Fee as a percentage, such as 2.5 or 3.75.
  • 3
    Enter the daily Galvanized Coil Price per kilogram in Toman.
  • 4
    Enter the sold Tonnage in tons. The calculator updates automatically.

Total Toman = Coil Price × Tonnage × 1000

Gross USD = Total Toman ÷ USD Rate

Fee USD = Gross USD × Fee %

Net USD = Gross USD − Fee USD
